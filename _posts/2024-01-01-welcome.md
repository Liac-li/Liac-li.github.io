This blog will collect research thoughts, paper reading notes, experiment logs, and ideas that are not yet polished enough for a formal paper.

The style is intentionally lightweight: a chronological list of Markdown posts, each focused on one question or one small observation.

## Possible Directions

- Notes on papers I am reading
- Short experiment writeups
- Open questions around agents and model evaluation
- Reflections on research tooling
