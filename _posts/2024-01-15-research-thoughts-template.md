Use this post as a template when writing future research notes.

## Question

What is the core question or confusion behind this note?

## Observation

Write the main observation in plain language. A good note should make the idea easy to revisit months later.

## Evidence

List papers, experiments, or examples that support the observation.

## Open Problems

- What is still unclear?
- What would be worth testing next?
- What would change your mind?
